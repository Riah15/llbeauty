About the Company

Locs and Lashes is a beauty salon based in Winter Haven, FL that focuses on servicing women through hair, and facial services. This salon strives to make sure women feel welcome in our environment and provide a place of relaxation from their daily activites.

Who Is Locs and Lashes

Mariah Brown-Tullis is the proud owner and CEO of Locs and Lashes Beauty Salon. Channeling her love of nails and hair from youth, she decided to pursue a career in Cosmetology and received licensing from Ridge Technical College in Winter Haven, FL. Graduating from South Florida with a B.S. in Graphic Design, Mariah enhances the branding of Locs and Lashes through web-design and marketing. Along with her love for cosmetology, Mariah also loves fellowshipping with women and believes in the power of female encouragement. In addition to beautifying women, Locs and Lashes Salon is a place where women unify and build one another. The goal of the salon is to make women beautiful, focusing on making them feel as beautiful on the outside as they are on the inside.

Mission Statement

Clean Salon. Celebrity Service. Confident Customer.

Vision Statement

Our vision statement is to fulfill the needs of every customer, helping to bring their ideas to life. We strive to make our customers feel as beautiful on the outisde as they are on the inside, so that they leave the salon satisfied and full of confidence.
